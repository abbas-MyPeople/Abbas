# MARKETING_COPY — AZ Social Tables

Binding positioning rules (from the owner):
- ❌ "Join our dating app." → ✅ "Houston singles: dinner with real people this Thursday."
- ❌ "AI matchmaking platform." → ✅ "One table. Real food. Real conversation."
- Restaurant line: **"We turn empty tables into curated social dining experiences."**
- Never lead with dating. Dating is one format of four. The word "app" never appears
  in consumer copy. No startup-bro tone; real revenue, real people, real food.

---

## Consumer positioning

**Promise:** Meet real people over real food.

**Elevator:** We host small, curated dinners at great local restaurants — singles
tables, new-friends tables, chef's tastings, community nights. You apply for a seat,
we balance the table, a host breaks the ice, and then it's just a good dinner. No
profiles, no swiping, no chat rooms. Your deposit comes off your food bill.

**Proof points to always include:** public restaurant · host present all evening ·
every guest name-verified and hand-approved · deposit credits toward the meal ·
12–20 seats, never a crowd.

## Restaurant positioning

**Promise:** Fill slow restaurant nights with pre-booked local customers.

**Elevator:** We turn empty tables into curated social dining experiences. We recruit
and vet the guests, collect deposits so they actually show up, host the whole evening,
and hand you a one-page recap the next morning — covers, average spend, revenue vs.
your normal night. You pay a flat fee per *seated* guest. No-shows and empty chairs
cost you nothing. First event free.

**Contrast line:** Delivery apps take ~30% and keep the customer. We bring full-price
dine-in covers who fall in love with your dining room — and come back with the people
they met at your table.
