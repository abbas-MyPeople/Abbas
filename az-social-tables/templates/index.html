How it works

Apply for a seat

Two minutes: who you are, what you're looking for, what you eat. No account, no profile.

We curate the table

We balance every dinner by hand — age range, intent, group mix — so it actually works. You'll hear back within 48 hours.

Reserve with a small deposit

Deposits (usually $10) hold your seat and come off your food bill. It keeps the table full of people who show up.

Come to dinner

A host greets you, seats you, breaks the ice, and steps back. Order what you like. Stay as long as you want.

Trust & safety, plainly

  • Every dinner is in a public restaurant with our host present the whole time.
  • We collect names and phone numbers — everyone at the table is accountable.
  • House rules: be kind, show up, no hard selling, no photographing other guests without asking.
  • Anyone who makes the table feel unsafe is removed and not invited back.
  • Your info is used to run the dinner you applied for — never sold.
