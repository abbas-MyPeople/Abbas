SocialTables

Curated dinners at local restaurants — real-world dining, not a dating app. A pilot by AZ Restaurant Partners, Spring TX.

How it works

Apply for a seat → we curate a balanced table → dinner at a great local restaurant. Deposits go toward your meal. A host is present at every table.

House rules

Be kind, show up, no hard selling, no recording other guests. Public venues only. We remove anyone who makes the table feel unsafe — no refund needed on our end, no debate.
