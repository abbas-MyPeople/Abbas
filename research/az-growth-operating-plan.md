## The uncontested position AZ can own (the wedge)
Ranking content today is either **vendor blogs** (Toast/Owner/Rezku — structurally biased) or **marketing
agencies** (retainer social/ads, not tech advisory). **Nobody owns "a vendor-neutral, operator-founded (ex-
Google engineer who owns an award-winning restaurant), no-kickbacks, honest-math" voice.** Every asset
should lean into that: real numbers, "when NOT to buy this," total cost of ownership, and the founder story.

## The build patterns (repeatable)
1. **Calculators** = free result on-page + (later) email-gated PDF detail → link magnet + 30–50% lead opt-in.
2. **"How much does X really cost (2026)"** cost-hub pages → highest commercial intent; lead with a 40–60
   word extractable answer (AEO), then a real TCO table.
3. **Programmatic** "<Tool> pricing" / "<Tool> alternatives" / "<A> vs <B>" from the 100-tool directory.
4. **AEO everywhere:** FAQPage + Article/HowTo + LocalBusiness/Service + author (founder E-E-A-T) schema;
   question-headers; hard numbers with citations (Princeton GEO: +30–40% AI visibility); original data.
5. **Conversion off-ramp on every page:** calculator/checklist + "2-min fit finder" alongside "book a call"
   (68% of B2B buyers won't book on first touch — convert them during research), risk-reversal at every CTA.
