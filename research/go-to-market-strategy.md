# Go-To-Market Strategy — AZ Restaurant Partners (post-deploy)

*Synthesis of three research streams (paid ads · cold outbound · organic/community/partnerships), tailored to: a solo founder who is himself a respected, profitable restaurant owner (Wok & Karahi, Spring TX), living in Cypress TX, deeply tied to the Houston South-Asian/Muslim/immigrant restaurant community, selling a done-for-you, **pay-only-after-you-save** service with a "Founding 5" pilot. Reliability tags inside the source docs: research/funnel-strategy.md and the three channel deep-dives.*

---

## The one-line verdict

**For this founder, trust beats attention.** The data is blunt: in this buyer segment referrals/word-of-mouth dominate (84% of B2B buyers start with a referral; referrals ~5× the sales of paid ads at <⅓ the cost), and the founder's single biggest asset — *being one of them* — only works face-to-face and inside the community. So **discovery should begin with feet and relationships, not ad spend.** Paid ads and cold outbound are real, but they are **supporting channels to fill the first 5 and seed the flywheel**, not the engine.

**Priority order to start:**
1. **In-person, slow-hours walk-ins** (his #1-converting channel) +
2. **Community / WhatsApp owner networks + referral seeding** (where this buyer lives) +
3. **The "real teardown" content + case-study flywheel** (compounds the other two)
4. *Then* **Meta click-to-WhatsApp ads** (to fill Founding 5 faster + build retargeting/lookalike seeds)
5. **Compliant manual cold calls + cold email** as outbound reinforcement
6. **Never cold-text** (Texas SB 140 — see legal guardrails)

---

## How discovery should BEGIN (the first 5, weeks 1–8)

The goal of phase one is **not** scale — it's to land the **Founding 5**, deliver measurable saves, and turn them into documented, in-language proof. Everything after that is the flywheel.

### Channel 1 — In-person walk-ins (the #1 channel for THIS founder)
- His owner credibility is wasted on a phone and maximized in person. Field/in-person sales convert far better than digital, and his offer ("let me into your numbers") *requires* trust.
- **When:** the 2:00–4:30 p.m. lull (never during rush). **Volume:** 8–12 quality drop-ins/day in one tight ZIP cluster (batch by ZIP to minimize driving). Expect to actually talk to an owner at ~40–60% of stops — a connect rate no other channel matches.
- **Approach:** lead with identity ("I'm Abbas, I own Wok & Karahi in Spring — a fellow owner, not a vendor"), ask one curiosity question ("how much are the apps taking from you?"), let them vent, then offer the **free 15-min walkthrough** + **pay-after-you-save / Founding 5**. Goal of the visit = book the walkthrough, not a same-day close.
- **Leave-behind:** a one-pager — his face + "Owner, Wok & Karahi," the **full A-to-Z span** (stop the bleed → win back & grow → see clearly & stay ahead, ~12 concrete capabilities), the pay-after-you-save offer, and a **QR code → the finder/booking link**. Lead with breadth (the whole tech+strategy team), not a narrow fix-list. A business card alone is forgettable.
- **Start in the dense halal/South-Asian corridor** (Hillcroft / Mahatma Gandhi District, Sugar Land) where his peer credibility lands hardest, plus his home ZIP clusters (Spring 77373/77379/77388/77389; Cypress 77429/77433/77410; NW Houston 77040/77064/77065/77070/77095).

### Channel 2 — Community + WhatsApp + referral seeding (~$0, highest leverage)
- This community runs on **WhatsApp owner groups**, ethnic chambers (Pakistan Chamber of Commerce-USA, Indo-American Chamber IACCGH, South Asian Chamber SACC Houston), and mosque/cultural networks (ISGH). Abbas is likely already in some groups; getting added to more via existing relationships is the single highest-leverage move.
- **How to seed:** never lead with the link. Lead with a *result* from his own restaurant ("here's the one report that showed me where my delivery money was going"), let owners ask, then hand them the **finder link owner-to-owner**. Always anchor on pay-after-you-save to kill the "another vendor" reflex.
- **Referral loop:** ask every early supporter for **2–3 warm intros** into their own cuisine/neighborhood circle. Currency is reciprocity, not cash (fits the no-kickback ethos): e.g., "I'll do your next quarter's review free for 3 intros who become clients."

### Channel 3 — The "real teardown" content + case-study flywheel (compounding)
- His unfair advantage: document real money-leaks and fixes from **his own restaurant and his clients** (Gary-Vee build-in-public). Short vertical video (Reels/TikTok/Shorts), **3 batch-filmed clips/week**.
- Pillars: (1) money-leak teardowns ("here's where this spot lost $4,200 last month" — the hero, drives the finder link), (2) behind-the-scenes at Wok & Karahi, (3) client wins / in-language UGC, (4) the direct offer (Founding 5 / free finder).
- **The flywheel (how 5 clients become 50):** close a Founding-5 client → deliver a measurable save → document a one-page, numbers-forward case study **+ a 30–60s in-language video testimonial** filmed in their restaurant → **re-inject it into that cuisine/neighborhood's own owner network** (their WhatsApp/Facebook groups, chamber, masjid circle) → ask for 2–3 intros at peak goodwill → repeat per cuisine vertical. One win, reused across content, GBP reviews, and PR.

## How discovery SCALES (weeks 4+, once foundations + first proof exist)

### Channel 4 — Meta ads (the right *paid* first move; supporting, not primary)
- **Owner-targeting is indirect now:** Meta gutted detailed targeting in 2025 and pushes broad audience + strong conversion signal. Use **geo as the sharpest filter** (5–10 mi pins on Spring/Cypress/NW Houston/Katy/Tomball — only advertise where he can drive for the walkthrough), a light "Page Admins / Small business owners" layer, and **1% lookalikes off finder visitors/leads** once there are ~100. **Let the creative do the targeting** (copy only an owner cares about).
- **Best format = Click-to-WhatsApp ads** — native to this buyer, the qualification chat is free (72-hr window; first ~1,000 conversations/mo free), and it sets up the in-person walkthrough naturally. Secondary: finder-page conversion (highest-intent, retargetable) + Instant Forms (autofill off / SMS-verify for quality).
- **Creative:** founder-to-camera Reels, lead with the **"$79k/yr in commissions"** number + **"you only pay after we save you money"** + Founding-5 scarcity. PAS structure.
- **Budget:** ~$20–30/day, commit **$1,000–$1,500 to *learn*** over 4–6 weeks; judge on **booked walkthroughs**, not vanity leads. Always run a ~20% retargeting campaign on finder visitors.
- **Expected:** ~1–3 booked walkthroughs/week in a good week once settled; high CAC (high-hundreds/signed restaurant) — fine for a high-value performance engagement, and it still earns its keep by building lookalike seeds + creative learnings.
- **Skip Google Local Services Ads (ineligible).** A tiny ($5–15/day) Google Search campaign on commission/POS terms with heavy negatives is optional demand-capture *after* Meta works.

### Channel 5 — Compliant manual cold calls + cold email (outbound reinforcement)
- **Calls:** manual dial, published business landlines, the 2–4:30 window; ~8 dials per reach, owners often answer themselves. Peer + money-leak + risk-reversal + specific-time-ask script. Reach most on attempt 2–4.
- **Email:** separate sending domain, SPF/DKIM/DMARC, 3-week warmup; 4-touch value-first sequence (honest 3–5 word subjects, one real personalization, breakup last); ~8% reply with follow-ups. CAN-SPAM footer (physical address + opt-out).
- **Best combined play:** **visit → leave one-pager w/ QR → same-night "great meeting you" email → call 2–3 days later → breakup email.** Multi-touch is what converts.

### Trust intermediaries / partnerships (parallel, low cost)
- People these owners already trust weekly: **food distributors & halal suppliers** (Restaurant Depot, Green Valley/Hafiz Brothers), **POS resellers/installers**, **restaurant bookkeepers/accountants**, **strip-mall landlords**, and **associations** (Greater Houston chapter of the Texas Restaurant Association — speaking slots > vendor booth).
- **No-kickback reciprocal referrals** (the trust moat): AZ refers clean business to best-fit vendors and keeps no commission; they refer owners who need strategy. Offer partners a **co-branded finder** to hand out.

### Earned PR / ethnic media (once 1–2 case studies exist)
- **Voice of Asia**, **Indo American News**, **Masala Radio/KBRZ** cover community business-success stories. Angle: "a Houston owner helping immigrant family restaurants stop the bleed." Free editorial, lands in front of the buyer in-language.

## Legal guardrails (set once, non-negotiable)
- **Manual calls only**, published **business landlines**, **9am–9pm**, no prerecorded/autodialer. **No cold texts** — Texas **SB 140** (eff. Sep 1, 2025) treats texts as solicitation with a private right of action + treble damages; A2P 10DLC blocks unregistered texts anyway. Only text after consent.
- **Cold email is legal** (CAN-SPAM, opt-out regime, no B2B exception): honest headers/subject, **physical postal address**, clear opt-out honored within 10 business days.
- Keep **one master "do-not-contact" list**; honor any opt-out across all channels within 10 business days.
- Before *scaling* phone outreach, **30 min with a Texas TCPA/SB-140 attorney** to confirm the manual model doesn't trigger registration.

## Free foundations to set up the week of launch (prerequisites)
1. **Meta Pixel + one-click Conversions API** on the finder page — fire events on the **result/leak-number screen** (`LeakCalculated`, `Lead`). (~17.8% lower cost/result vs pixel-only.)
2. **UTMs** on every placement of the finder link (`utm_source`, `utm_campaign`, `utm_content=hook`).
3. **WhatsApp Business** with quick replies + away message (CTWA underperforms if owners get silence).
4. **Google Business Profile** for AZ (categories: Marketing Agency / Business Management Consultant) — free, captures search + hosts Founding-5 reviews.
5. The **one-page leave-behind** (QR → finder/booking).
6. A focused **list of ~150 independent restaurants** in target ZIPs (Google Maps scrape → website email enrichment → owner names from site/SOS/TABC → cross-check new openings via Harris County / City of Houston food permits). Flag independents on 2+ delivery apps with a website.

## The two highest-leverage things to get right
1. **Every Founding-5 win must be measured, documented, in-language, and re-injected into the winner's own community.** That single discipline turns his reputation + first 5 clients into a near-zero-cost acquisition machine.
2. **Defend two funnel metrics:** finder **completion → lead capture** (the ~40% interactive-quiz advantage) and **booked → showed** (an SMS/WhatsApp reminder sequence — the build noted below).
