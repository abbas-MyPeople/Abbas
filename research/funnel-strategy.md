# Sales Funnel Strategy — Research & Recommended Funnel for AZ Restaurant Partners

*Deep research synthesis. Reliability tags: [PRACTITIONER] = the named expert's own material (Hormozi/Gary Vee). [IND] = independent press/academic. [SOFT] = vendor/marketing blog (self-interested — treat magnitudes as directional, not precise).*

**Business in one line:** AZ helps independent restaurants stop bleeding money (delivery commissions, missed calls, disconnected tools, no strategy). Top of funnel = a free interactive "Toolbox / Money-Leak Finder" → free call → "Founding 5" performance-based pilot (paid only after the restaurant saves money).

**Headline takeaway:** Nearly every authoritative framework points to the *exact* funnel AZ is already planning. The Finder is, almost textbook, the highest-converting category of lead magnet (an interactive diagnostic that reveals a problem). The performance pilot is the strongest category of risk-reversal Hormozi describes. The work is about *structuring* it correctly.

## 1. Alex Hormozi — the offer & the lead magnet

### Value Equation
**Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) ÷ (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice).** Leverage is in the *denominator* — collapse time and effort toward zero and value explodes. Value is *perceived*: if you don't communicate the benefit, the buyer doesn't feel it. [SOFT/IND; YAP E199 [PRACTITIONER]]

**For AZ — score the offer on all four:**
- **Dream outcome:** not "we optimize your tools" → *"Keep the money you're already making."* Quantify it: a restaurant running $20K/mo through apps at ~33% loses **~$79,200/yr** in commissions alone. That number *is* the dream outcome — put it on the result page.
- **Perceived likelihood:** the Founding 5 performance pilot ("you only pay after you save") pushes this toward 100% — the buyer literally cannot lose. Lead with it.
- **Time delay:** the Finder gives an *instant* personalized result. The pilot should show a "first save" fast (recover a missed-call source, cut one commission tier in weeks 2–4). Advertise a fast first win.
- **Effort & sacrifice:** owners are time-starved and low-tech → everything done-for-you; quiz <2 min; pilot requires almost nothing from the owner.

### Grand Slam Offer + guarantee
Guarantee types: unconditional (money-back), conditional (defined outcome), **performance** (you don't get paid until the result happens), implied (reputation). AZ's "paid only after you save" pilot *is the strongest type that exists* — a **performance guarantee**. Frame and name it: *"You don't pay us a cent until we've put money back in your pocket. If you don't save, you don't pay. Period."* Stack scarcity: *"We only take 5 restaurants."* [SOFT/IND summaries of $100M Offers]

### $100M Leads — lead magnets
- Four types: software/tools, information, services, physical. Hormozi's own software example: *"a dashboard that gives gym owners their stats vs. industry averages."*
- **"Give away the how, sell the done-for-you."** Over-deliver so people feel obligated to pay for execution.
- **The magnet must REVEAL a problem your core offer then solves** (Problem-Solution Cycle): "a complete solution to a narrow problem that, once solved, reveals another problem solved by your core offer."
- 7-step framework: (1) define narrow problem, (2) solve it for real, (3) pick format, (4) test the name, (5) make it easy, (6) over-deliver, (7) CTA with a reason to act *now* (scarcity/urgency).
- Core Four ways to get leads: warm outreach, posting content, cold outreach, paid ads. [IND summaries — manassaloi, jonathanrintala, shortform]

**For AZ:** The Finder is a *software lead magnet that reveals a problem* — the single best-fitting category. It solves "which of 100+ tools fit me?" and in doing so reveals the bigger problem (you're losing money across delivery/calls/disconnected tools with no strategy) — exactly what the pilot solves. Name it for the outcome: **"Money-Leak Finder" tests stronger than "Toolbox Finder."** End with a scarcity CTA (Founding 5 — 5 spots).

## 2. Gary Vaynerchuk — the content engine that feeds the link

- **Jab, Jab, Jab, Right Hook:** give value 3+ times before the ask. [SOFT/IND]
- **Native content** per platform; don't post identical everywhere.
- **"Document, don't create" + pillar/reverse-pyramid:** one long-form pillar → dozens of platform-native micro-clips pointing back to the link. Documented: one keynote → 30+ pieces → ~35M views. [PRACTITIONER — garyvaynerchuk.com]

**For AZ — a documentation engine, not a campaign:**
- **Jabs:** free specific content — "how DoorDash's 3 tiers actually cost you," "3 missed-call setups that lose $2K/mo," "the 5 tools every taco shop needs." Every 3–4 value posts, one right hook → the Finder link.
- **Pillar:** a weekly *real restaurant teardown* ("I found $4,300/mo for this pho shop — here's how") sliced into Reels/TikTok/Shorts + carousel + text. Documenting the Founding-5 pilots IS the content (proof + marketing in one).
- **Native + local;** bio link = the Finder, always.

## 3. Core funnel mechanics

**Stages:** Awareness → Lead capture → Nurture → Booked call → Close → Retain/Referral. Referred customers retain **37% better** and refer **4× more.** [SOFT/IND — Salesforce/Gong/Sybill]

**Quiz / interactive lead magnet — the headline finding:**
- Quiz funnels convert **~40.1%** of starters vs. **~6.6% median landing page**; **~65% completion**. [SOFT — tryinteract Quiz Conversion Report]
- B2B services quizzes avg **~38.5%**. Static PDFs **3–10%**; calculators **~50%**; quizzes **20–40%**. Interactive assets lift list growth ~6.2× vs. static (HubSpot, cited). [SOFT — amraandelma, digitalapplied]
- Diagnostic flow that works: answer questions → instant score/result → tailored recommendations → book a consult. Reveals specific problems, qualifies the lead, makes the call the natural next step. [SOFT — orrjo, nusparkprofit]

**Landing page best practices:** [SOFT — Shopify/CrazyEgg/Unbounce/Bitly]
- One single CTA / one goal.
- Cut form friction: 11→4 fields = **+120%**; CTA copy "Sign up free"→"Trial for free" = **+104%**.
- **83% of landing traffic is mobile** — build mobile-first (owners open forwarded links on their phone).
- Authentic urgency/scarcity, not manipulative.

**Booked-call show rates:** [SOFT — roezan/default/salescaptain]
- Automated reminders cut no-shows **up to 50%.** Sequence: instant confirmation → ~48h reminder → 2–4h nudge. **>90% of texts opened in minutes** — SMS is the channel.
- Two-way SMS confirm ("reply Y"), optional pre-call confirmation call. Qualify before the calendar link. Send agenda + 3 outcomes.

**Email/SMS nurture:** 4–7 emails over 2–3 weeks (2–3 days apart, then widening) + 1–2 SMS. Greet → free value → introduce solution → social proof → beat objections. [SOFT — cognism/mailreach/mixmax]

**For AZ:** Choosing an interactive quiz over a PDF is worth a **~4–6× conversion advantage** — the biggest single lever. Gate one short form (email + phone, not 11 fields), mobile-first, single CTA on the result page, then an SMS-driven 48h/2-4h show-rate sequence.

## 4. Funnel for THIS buyer — local, independent, often immigrant/family-run

The generic SaaS funnel (ad → demo → self-serve trial) does **not** fit. This buyer runs on trust, peer proof, and messaging apps:
- **Word of mouth dominates:** **78% of diners choose by a friend's recommendation**; peer trust carries into how owners pick *vendors* too. [SOFT/IND — TapMango/Toast/OpenTable]
- **WhatsApp/WeChat is the channel.** Owner communities live there; referral mechanics must be *easy and informal*, not formal forms. [SOFT — waakif/reelo/servmeco]
- **The "money belongs to the platform, not you" pain is specific and real:** third-party order customer data sits in DoorDash/Uber Eats — you can't email, text, or add them to loyalty. Margins 3–9%; 30% on a $50 order can be net-negative; ~$79,200/yr at $20K/mo, 33%. *This is the language that resonates.* [SOFT/IND — getsauce/activemenus/cloudkitchens]

**For AZ:**
- **Trust is the gate, not information** — front-load proof: a real local restaurant's name + saved-dollar number, owner video testimonials in-language, "we only get paid if you save."
- **Build the Finder to be forwarded in WhatsApp/WeChat groups** — the standalone link is the asset that travels owner-to-owner. Result page needs a one-tap **"Share with another owner" (WhatsApp)** button — referrals are this segment's #1 channel.
- **Capture phone, follow up by call/text** (many won't self-serve a calendar); offer to come by in person.
- **Language:** result page + follow-ups in dominant local languages (we already ship EN/HI/UR).
- **Hyper-local proof loop:** each Founding-5 win → documented case in that cuisine/neighborhood → shared into that community's owner groups → next 5 leads.

## 5. The Finder as a lead magnet — maximizing it

- **Gate the RESULT, not the start.** Starters convert ~40%, ~65% finish. Let them answer first (builds investment), then gate the result behind a 2-field form (email + phone). Fewer fields = far higher conversion.
- **Result page must:** (1) show a personalized tool stack (the free "how"); (2) **reveal the bigger problem with a number** ("you're likely losing ~$X,X00/mo to commissions + missed calls"); (3) single CTA "Book your free Money-Leak Call — claim 1 of 5 Founding spots" + performance-guarantee line under it; (4) local same-cuisine social proof; (5) one-tap WhatsApp share.
- **Hand off to the call:** instant SMS confirmation → 48h + 2-4h reminders → two-way "reply Y" → pre-call text with 3 outcomes. Lightly qualify in the quiz so the list is warm.
- **Track the standalone link:** unique UTM per placement; funnel metrics: clicks → starts → completions → captures → booked → shown → closed → $ saved → referrals. Pixel the result page for retargeting quiz-completers who didn't book.

## Recommended funnel — stage by stage

**Offer framing:** *"The Founding 5 — we find the money your restaurant is losing, set up the right tools, and you don't pay a cent until we've put cash back in your pocket. Only 5 spots."* (Performance guarantee + scarcity + collapsed risk = near-100% perceived likelihood, near-zero owner effort.)

- **Stage 0 — Content engine (Gary Vee):** weekly pillar = real teardown video → 5–10 native clips; jab-jab-jab value, every 3–4 posts one right hook → Finder link; bio link = Finder; document the pilots.
- **Stage 1 — Awareness → click:** organic short-form (primary), warm/in-person outreach, forwarded links in owner WhatsApp/WeChat groups. All roads → the standalone Finder link.
- **Stage 2 — Lead magnet:** the Finder. <2 min, mobile-first, multilingual. Answer first → gate result behind email + phone. ~38–40% of starters become leads (vs ~6.6% static).
- **Stage 3 — Result page:** personalized stack → "$X/mo you're likely losing" → single CTA (Founding 5) + guarantee line → local testimonial → WhatsApp share.
- **Stage 4 — Nurture (non-bookers):** 4–7 emails / 2–3 weeks + 1–2 SMS: greet → value → introduce Founding 5 → case study → objections → scarcity nudge.
- **Stage 5 — Show rate:** instant SMS confirm → 48h → 2-4h → two-way confirm → optional bilingual pre-call. Cut no-shows up to 50%.
- **Stage 6 — Call → Founding 5 close:** quantify the biggest leak live → annual cost (~$79K framing) → present performance pilot → scarcity ("3 of 5 left"). Risk fully reversed.
- **Stage 7 — Retain → referral flywheel:** fast first win → documented $ saved → case-study video → in-language testimonial → shared into community owner groups → seeds next 5. Informal referral reward. Should eventually outproduce paid acquisition.

**Two highest-leverage metrics to defend:** quiz completion → lead capture (the ~40% advantage), and booked → shown (the SMS sequence).
