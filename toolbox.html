POS 7

Toast

from $69/mo + hardware

All-in-one restaurant OS: POS, ordering, KDS, payroll.

Best for One connected system instead of a dozen disconnected tools.

Watch out Hardware lock-in; all-in cost often $1,000+/mo.

Square for Restaurants

$0–149/mo

iPad POS unified with Square payments and ecosystem.

Best for Affordable, easy starting point that just works.

Watch out Locked to Square processing; thin for complex coursing.

Lightspeed Restaurant

$69–399/mo + payments

Cloud POS with deep inventory, analytics, multi-location tools.

Best for Strong inventory and reporting for serious operators.

Watch out Higher tiers pricey for a tiny shop.

SpotOn

$0–135/mo + payments

Restaurant POS plus marketing, loyalty, reviews and strong support.

Best for POS bundled with guest-marketing and great support.

Watch out Must use SpotOn hardware/processing; early-switch fee.

TouchBistro

from $69/mo

iPad POS purpose-built for tableside full-service.

Best for Built for tableside, coursing, and floor management.

Watch out Add-ons inflate cost; weak for high-volume QSR.

Clover

~$15–90/mo/device + processing

Flexible POS hardware and app marketplace, sold via banks/resellers.

Best for Affordable, customizable POS with a big app store.

Watch out Reseller pricing varies wildly; rates depend on merchant account.

Revel Systems

from $99/mo/terminal + ~$674 install

iPad POS for high-volume and multi-unit QSR/chains.

Best for Robust POS built for volume and many locations.

Watch out 3-yr contract + 2-terminal minimum; high setup.
