Mirus

Custom multi-unit data warehousing and report building.

est. ~$ enterprise

Visit Mirus website →

What it costs

Enterprise Unlimited at $95 per unit per month (unlimited data sources, users and custom reports). Minimum fee under 20 units; volume discounts begin around 100 units.

Best for

Multi-unit operators with complex data who need a custom warehouse and flexible, code-free reporting.

Pros

  • Integrates POS, labor, payroll, inventory and accounting into one warehouse
  • Unlimited users and custom reports for a flat per-unit fee
  • Code-free ad-hoc report writer
  • Established since 1999 with deep restaurant focus

Cons

  • Needs analyst time to build and maintain reports
  • Chain-oriented; minimum fee under 20 units
  • Less turnkey than dashboard-first tools

Integrations

Builds a custom data warehouse pulling from POS, labor, payroll, inventory, accounting and spreadsheets.

Alternatives

Avero · Tenzo (BI) · Restaurant365

Our take: A flexible, transparently priced data warehouse for multi-unit groups that want tailored reporting; you'll need someone willing to build the reports to get full value.
