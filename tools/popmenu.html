Popmenu

AI phone + text answering plus interactive menu and marketing.

~$200–499/mo

Visit Popmenu website →

What it costs

Tiered subscription: from ~$179/mo (or ~$159/mo annual) up to Essentials ~$299/mo and Premier ~$499/mo, plus add-ons — AI marketing ~$300/mo, online ordering ~$50/mo (+~$1/order to the guest), and ~$300/mo per extra location. SMS volume is capped by tier.

Best for

Independents and small groups wanting an engaging interactive menu plus AI phone/text answering and marketing in one bundle.

Pros

  • Interactive menu plus AI phone/text answering
  • Built-in marketing: email, SMS, reviews, promotions
  • Website and digital menus included
  • All-in-one guest engagement suite

Cons

  • Marketing-first, not ordering-first (ordering is an add-on)
  • Add-ons (AI marketing, ordering, extra locations) stack the cost
  • SMS messages capped by plan
  • Hard to buy just one piece a la carte

Integrations

Integrates with major POS systems and online-ordering/delivery tools; first-party website, menus, AI answering, and marketing.

Alternatives

BentoBox · Owner.com · Slang.ai

Our take: A capable engagement bundle if you'll use the interactive menu plus marketing and AI answering together — just price the add-ons, since the base plan is only the start.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
