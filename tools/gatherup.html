GatherUp

Generates reviews and drafts on-brand replies.

$99/mo (single)

Visit GatherUp website →

What it costs

Published rates: $99/mo for a single location, or about $60/mo per location for 2-10 locations, dropping toward $25/mo per location at 100+ (higher tiers quoted). Includes ~3,000 email and ~300 SMS credits per location monthly. Add-ons: Listings Hub ~$40/mo per location, promotional SMS ~$10/mo per location.

Best for

Independents and small groups that want transparent, affordable review generation with on-brand reply help.

Pros

  • Publishes its prices upfront, which is rare in this category
  • Generates reviews and drafts replies you can approve
  • Email and SMS credits included in the base plan
  • Per-location cost drops sharply as you add sites

Cons

  • Add-ons (listings, promo SMS) push the real cost higher
  • Less restaurant-specific than survey-first tools
  • Bigger brands see less rating-to-revenue lift than independents

Integrations

Connects to Google and other review/listing platforms; offers white-label options for agencies.

Alternatives

Marqii · Birdeye · Podium

Our take: One of the few review tools that shows its pricing, and it's fair for a small operator. For an independent, a one-star rating lift can mean real revenue, so the payback is easy to justify.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
