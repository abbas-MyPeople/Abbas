Loyalty & CRM

Como

AI-personalized loyalty with branded apps and deep POS integration.

est. ~$100–300/mo/loc

Visit Como website →

What it costs

Quote-only; existing estimates put it roughly $100-$300/mo per location, depending on features and number of locations. No public tiers; price set after a sales consultation.

Best for

Restaurants wanting AI-personalized, gamified loyalty and a branded app without jumping to full enterprise scale.

Pros

  • Personalized, data-driven offers (cashback, spin-to-win, targeted deals)
  • Branded app and deep POS integration
  • Gamified mechanics that drive engagement
  • Scales from single sites to multi-unit

Cons

  • Quote-only pricing requires a sales process
  • Can be more platform than a very small shop needs
  • Setup and configuration take some effort

Integrations

Deep POS integrations; positioned for food-and-beverage operators across single and multi-unit.

Alternatives

TapMango · Incentivio · Paytronix

Our take: A strong middle option: more personalization and gamification than POS-native loyalty, without the enterprise price of Paytronix or Thanx. The catch is you'll need a quote to evaluate it.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
