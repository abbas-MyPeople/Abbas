Incentivio

Ordering + loyalty + marketing with white-label apps.

from ~$249/mo

Visit Incentivio website →

What it costs

Starts around $249/mo plus setup fees; higher tiers and custom pricing for multi-unit. Combines online ordering, loyalty, marketing automation, and analytics in one platform.

Best for

Multi-unit restaurants that want a branded app, ordering, and loyalty unified in one commission-free platform.

Pros

  • White-label app and web ordering plus loyalty in one system
  • Points-, visit-, or spend-based rewards with tiers
  • Marketing automation and guest analytics included
  • Commission-free ordering keeps more of each sale

Cons

  • Setup fees on top of the monthly cost
  • Depth and price overshoot most single-unit operators
  • Best ROI requires meaningful digital-order volume

Integrations

Integrates with POS systems and delivery/ordering channels; built to centralize digital ordering and guest data.

Alternatives

Como · Thanx · Paytronix

Our take: A solid all-in-one for small chains wanting their own ordering app plus loyalty without enterprise pricing. A single independent should weigh the setup fee and monthly against simpler options.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
