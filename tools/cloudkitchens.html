CloudKitchens

Ghost-kitchen real estate plus tech to run delivery brands.

est. ~$ lease + fees (quote)

Visit CloudKitchens website →

What it costs

Lease-based ghost-kitchen space, typically ~$3,000-$15,000/mo (often quoted from ~$4,300/mo) depending on market, plus utilities, deposits and platform fees. Leases can be as short as 12 months.

Best for

Delivery-only operators wanting turnkey kitchen space and tooling without signing a full restaurant lease.

Pros

  • Turnkey delivery-only kitchen space with shared amenities
  • Shorter lease terms than a traditional storefront
  • Lower upfront commitment than building out your own space
  • Included order-management tooling in some facilities

Cons

  • Requires committing to their facilities and locations
  • Opaque fee structure beyond base rent
  • Delivery-app commissions still apply on top

Integrations

Provides facility plus order-management tech; you still connect your own delivery-app accounts and brands.

Alternatives

CloudKitchens competitors (local commissaries) · Ghost Kitchen Brands · REEF Kitchens

Our take: A fast on-ramp to delivery-only operations without a storefront buildout, but read the fees carefully and remember app commissions still eat each order.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
