Sling

Shift scheduling, time-off and labor-cost management.

free–$3.40/user/mo

Visit Sling website →

What it costs

Free plan for up to 30 users (scheduling and communication). Premium ~$1.70/user/mo and Business ~$4/user/mo ($3.40 with annual billing); 15% off annual on paid plans. You only pay for users on the platform.

Best for

Single sites and small teams that want free or very cheap scheduling and labor-cost tracking.

Pros

  • Generous free tier (up to 30 users)
  • Very low per-user paid pricing
  • Shift swaps, time-off, and labor-cost tools
  • Cheap to scale as the team grows

Cons

  • Lighter forecasting and analytics than rivals
  • Free plan's mobile app is view-only
  • Less restaurant-specific than 7shifts

Integrations

Integrates with common POS and payroll tools; part of the Toast family of products.

Alternatives

Homebase · 7shifts · Deputy

Our take: The cheapest credible way to run scheduling for a small team, with a strong free tier. Step up to 7shifts or Deputy if you need real demand forecasting.
