Energy & maintenance

Powerhouse Dynamics Open Kitchen

IoT software unifying HVAC/lighting/equipment with AI energy mgmt.

est. ~$150–400/mo/loc

Visit Powerhouse Dynamics Open Kitchen website →

What it costs

Subscription as-a-service: a single monthly fee bundles hardware, installation, onboarding, managed services and software; no public figure (independent estimates ~$150-$400/mo per location). Vendor positions the fee as less than the energy savings delivered.

Best for

Multi-site foodservice operators wanting to monitor and control HVAC, refrigeration and equipment to cut energy use.

Pros

  • Connects and controls HVAC, refrigeration and kitchen equipment via IoT
  • Claimed energy savings up to ~20% and peak demand cuts up to ~15%
  • All-in monthly fee covers hardware, install and managed services
  • Adds temperature monitoring and sanitation compliance

Cons

  • Built for chains; overkill for a single location
  • Pricing not published; requires a quote
  • Requires equipment connectivity and install

Integrations

Connects a wide range of HVAC, refrigeration and kitchen equipment brands; includes DemandSmart electricity-cost AI.

Alternatives

GridPoint · Verdigris · Ecotrak

Our take: A proven energy-and-equipment platform that can pay for itself across a portfolio, but it's aimed at multi-site operators, not a single independent.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
