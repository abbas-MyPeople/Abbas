Deputy

Scheduling, demand forecasting and biometric clock-ins.

$5–9/user/mo

Visit Deputy website →

What it costs

Per user/month: Scheduling ~$4.50, Premium ~$6 (scheduling + time/attendance), Pro ~$9, plus Enterprise custom. Minimum ~$30/mo per invoice (from Sept 2025). Analytics+ add-on ~$1.50/user/mo. A weekly per-active-user option can lower cost for variable teams.

Best for

Fast-casual and full-service operators who want demand-based auto-scheduling at a low per-user price.

Pros

  • Low per-user entry pricing
  • Demand-based auto-scheduling and forecasting
  • Biometric/kiosk clock-ins and attendance
  • Flexible weekly billing for variable staffing

Cons

  • Per-user cost rises with large hourly teams
  • Minimum monthly spend introduced in 2025
  • Some advanced reporting is a paid add-on

Integrations

Integrates with major POS and payroll systems; broad app ecosystem.

Alternatives

7shifts · Homebase · Sling

Our take: Strong demand-based scheduling at a friendly per-user price for smaller teams. The math flips for large hourly rosters, where per-location tools may be cheaper.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
