Tattle

Causation-based guest surveys integrated with POS/loyalty.

from $59/mo/loc

Visit Tattle website →

What it costs

Per location per month, starting from $59/location, with lower per-location rates as you add sites. White-glove onboarding (setup specialist, custom surveys, integration, training) included; billing adjusts automatically as you add or remove locations.

Best for

Multi-unit fast-casual and full-service operators who want to pinpoint exactly what to fix to lift satisfaction.

Pros

  • Causation-based surveys tie feedback to specific operational drivers
  • Transparent per-location pricing from $59
  • Integrates with POS, ordering, and loyalty for higher response
  • Onboarding and mobile app included at no extra cost

Cons

  • Feedback/analytics focus, not public-review generation
  • Most valuable at multi-unit scale where you can act on patterns
  • Newer reputation/review-management features still maturing

Integrations

Connects to POS, online-ordering, and loyalty systems; integrates with platforms like Incentivio.

Alternatives

Ovation · GatherUp · Birdeye

Our take: The right tool when you want to know precisely what's dragging scores down, not just collect star ratings. Pair it with a review-generation tool if growing public reviews is also a goal.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
