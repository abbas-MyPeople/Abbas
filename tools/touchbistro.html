Visit TouchBistro website →

What it costs

iPad POS bundle from $69/mo; an Essentials bundle at ~$119/mo adds integrated payments and hardware with $0 upfront. Add-ons (KDS, inventory, online ordering, loyalty, reservations) are quote-only — TouchBistro no longer publishes their prices.

Best for

Full-service and fine-dining venues that want a POS purpose-built for tableside service, coursing, and floor management.

Pros

  • Designed specifically for tableside full-service
  • Strong coursing, table, and floor management
  • No commission on its own online orders
  • Works offline (local/hybrid setup)

Cons

  • Add-ons are quote-only and inflate the real cost
  • Weaker fit for high-volume QSR
  • Less transparent pricing than rivals
  • Smaller integration ecosystem

Integrations

Integrates with accounting, payroll, and delivery tools; first-party reservations, online ordering, loyalty, and KDS as paid add-ons.

Alternatives

Toast · Lightspeed Restaurant · SpotOn

Our take: A purpose-built full-service POS that shines tableside; just press for full add-on pricing before signing, since the headline $69 rarely reflects the real bill.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
