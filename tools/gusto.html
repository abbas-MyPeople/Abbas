Gusto

Full-service payroll, benefits and HR.

$49/mo + $6/employee

Visit Gusto website →

What it costs

Simple plan $49/mo + $6/employee; Plus $80/mo + $12/employee; Premium $180/mo + $22/employee. Contractor-only from $35/mo + $6/contractor. Add-ons (time tracking, benefits, multi-state) add $10-$30/employee.

Best for

Small to mid-size restaurants that want painless, full-service payroll, benefits and HR without restaurant-specific complexity.

Pros

  • Easy, full-service payroll with automatic tax filing
  • Transparent published pricing and a low base cost
  • Handles benefits, onboarding and contractor pay
  • Scales from a single shop to multi-location

Cons

  • Generic, not built for restaurant tips or POS-native hours
  • Per-employee and add-on fees stack with a large hourly team
  • Lacks deep tip-pooling features of restaurant-specific payroll

Integrations

Integrates with many POS, scheduling and accounting tools (including QuickBooks and various restaurant platforms).

Alternatives

Toast Payroll · 7shifts Payroll · Homebase Payroll

Our take: A reliable, easy payroll choice for most small restaurants; if tip pooling and POS-native hours are critical, a restaurant-specific payroll may serve you better.
