Notch

Digital supplier ordering, invoicing and AP across the supply chain.

est. ~$ quote

Visit Notch website →

What it costs

Quote-only. Marketplace per-order fees were discontinued in 2023; pricing now centers on its software modules (Order Manager, Invoice Manager, AP Manager). Contact sales for current rates.

Best for

Operators (especially in Canada) who want supplier ordering, invoicing, and AP automation in one place.

Pros

  • Centralizes supplier catalogs, ordering, and reorders
  • Automates invoice and AP workflows for better cash flow
  • Full line-of-sight into purchases and order status
  • No per-order marketplace fees

Cons

  • Strongest in Canada; US coverage thinner
  • Ordering/AP-focused, lighter on recipe costing
  • Opaque, quote-only pricing

Integrations

Works with existing distributor systems; connects restaurants and distributors plus AP/accounting workflows.

Alternatives

BlueCart · MarketMan · Ottimate (Plate IQ)

Our take: A clean choice for centralizing supplier ordering and accounts payable, particularly for Canadian operators. Get a quote and confirm your suppliers are on it before committing.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
