Recipe & food cost

xtraCHEF (by Toast)

Invoice OCR, line-item food cost and recipe analytics.

free tier–$349/mo

Visit xtraCHEF (by Toast) website →

What it costs

Free tier available; paid plans up to roughly $349/mo. Best value when paired with Toast POS.

Best for

Toast users (and others) who want invoice OCR and line-item food cost without a heavy back-office system.

Pros

  • Free tier makes starting on food-cost control low-risk
  • Automates invoice capture and line-item cost tracking
  • Recipe analytics tie costs back to menu items
  • Deep value if you already run Toast

Cons

  • Best value is really only realized on Toast
  • Less powerful for non-Toast multi-vendor shops
  • Not a full accounting or inventory platform on its own

Integrations

Tight integration with Toast POS; also accepts invoices from many vendors and exports to accounting tools.

Alternatives

MarginEdge · MarketMan · Parsley

Our take: A smart, low-risk first step into food-cost control, especially for Toast restaurants; standalone shops may find more neutral options like MarginEdge a better fit.
