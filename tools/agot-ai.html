Agot AI

Kitchen-camera computer vision for real-time order accuracy.

est. ~$500–1,500/mo/loc

Visit Agot AI website →

What it costs

Quote-only, enterprise/chain-oriented; no public pricing (independent estimates ~$500-$1,500/mo per location). Integration-heavy camera deployment.

Best for

QSR and fast-casual chains wanting kitchen-camera computer vision to catch order errors before they reach guests.

Pros

  • Computer vision spots 85%+ of order errors in real time
  • Real-time line-worker prompts down to the ingredient level
  • Also addresses food waste and speed of service
  • Catches mistakes before food leaves the kitchen

Cons

  • Chain-scale and integration-heavy to deploy
  • Requires kitchen camera installation
  • No public pricing; built for large operators

Integrations

Camera-based vision system integrated with kitchen workflow; real-time prompts to staff displays.

Alternatives

PreciTaste · Miso Robotics Flippy · Agot competitors (Kitchen vision tools)

Our take: Impressive accuracy tech that can cut remakes and complaints at scale, but the camera install and chain focus put it well beyond a single independent.
