Miso Robotics Flippy

AI robot arm that fries and hot-holds food.

~$3,500/mo + install

Visit Miso Robotics Flippy website →

What it costs

Robotics-as-a-Service (RaaS): roughly $3,000-$3,500/mo per unit on a multi-year contract, plus an installation fee (commonly cited around $11,000). The monthly fee bundles hardware, software updates, maintenance and support, so there's no big upfront purchase.

Best for

High-volume QSRs that run the fryer hard and want to take crew off the hottest, highest-turnover station.

Pros

  • No large capital outlay; predictable monthly fee covers service and upgrades
  • Frees staff from a dangerous, hard-to-staff station and runs consistently through a rush
  • Newer Flippy is smaller and faster, fitting into existing fry stations
  • Backed by major-chain pilots (White Castle, Jack in the Box)

Cons

  • Frying-focused; ROI only works with heavy, steady throughput
  • Multi-year contract plus install fee is a real commitment
  • Needs kitchen space and layout that can accommodate the arm

Integrations

Integrates with POS/kitchen systems for order data; installation and maintenance handled by Miso and partners.

Alternatives

Chef Robotics · Hyphen Makeline · PreciTaste

Our take: A genuine fix for the fryer staffing headache at high volume, but the contract and install cost mean it only pays off if your lane or counter is consistently slammed.
