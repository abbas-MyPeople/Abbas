Slang.ai

AI voice agent answers calls and books reservations 24/7.

$399–599/mo

Visit Slang.ai website →

What it costs

Flat monthly subscription: Core from ~$399/location/mo and Premium from ~$599/location/mo, with a custom enterprise tier; reported real-world cost runs ~$450–$600/location/mo. Unlimited call handling regardless of volume; multi-location groups can negotiate.

Best for

Full-service and fine-dining venues that lose booking and FAQ calls during the rush and want a polished 24/7 voice answer.

Pros

  • Answers calls 24/7 and books reservations
  • Handles FAQs, special requests, and concierge bypass
  • Reservation integrations (OpenTable, SevenRooms, Yelp)
  • Flat pricing regardless of call volume

Cons

  • Texts an order link rather than taking phone orders
  • Premium integrations/analytics cost more
  • $399+ floor is steep for very low call volume
  • Focused on bookings/FAQs more than ordering

Integrations

Integrates with OpenTable, SevenRooms, and Yelp reservations; sends order links rather than injecting orders into a POS.

Alternatives

Loman.ai · Newo.ai · Goodcall

Our take: A strong, polished AI answer for reservation- and FAQ-heavy restaurants — but if you need the phone to actually take orders, look at an ordering-first agent.
