Online ordering

Owner.com

AI-built website, app, ordering, loyalty and marketing in one.

$499/mo + 5% guest fee

Visit Owner.com website →

What it costs

Two month-to-month plans: Flex at $249/mo plus a 5% per-order fee, or Flat-Rate at $499/mo with 0% restaurant commission; guests pay a 5% order-support fee. No contracts; both plans include website, app, ordering, loyalty, and marketing.

Best for

Independents serious about winning commission-free repeat orders who do (or want to do) meaningful direct online volume.

Pros

  • AI-built website, branded app, ordering, loyalty, and marketing in one
  • Commission-free on the Flat-Rate plan
  • No long-term contract; cancel anytime
  • Built specifically to grow direct repeat orders

Cons

  • Flat $499 is steep at low order volume
  • Flex plan's 5% fee plus guest support fee adds up
  • 5% guest-facing fee can deter some customers
  • Best ROI only above ~$5k/mo in online sales

Integrations

POS integrations plus first-party website, app, loyalty, email/SMS, and SEO tooling; chargeback protection built in.

Alternatives

ChowNow · BentoBox · Popmenu

Our take: A strong all-in-one for independents focused on direct ordering — worth it once your online volume justifies the flat fee; otherwise start on the Flex plan.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
