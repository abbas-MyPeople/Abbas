Kitchen display (KDS)

Fresh KDS

Tablet-based kitchen display; works with Square/Clover and more.

$15–39/screen/mo

Visit Fresh KDS website →

What it costs

Subscription from about $16/mo per screen (existing estimate $15-$39/screen depending on tier). Runs on standard tablets (iOS 13+/Android 7+), so you can avoid proprietary hardware costs.

Best for

Independents and small shops that want an affordable, flexible kitchen display that works with their existing POS.

Pros

  • Very low per-screen pricing
  • Runs on off-the-shelf tablets, cutting hardware cost
  • Works with Square, Clover and other POS systems
  • Quick payback from fewer mistakes and faster tickets

Cons

  • Fewer enterprise/multi-unit controls
  • Lighter analytics than high-end KDS
  • You supply and maintain the tablets

Integrations

Integrates with Square, Clover, and other POS platforms; hardware-agnostic on common tablets.

Alternatives

Toast KDS · QSR Automations ConnectSmart · Square KDS

Our take: The best-value KDS for an independent: cheap, flexible, and POS-agnostic. If you need deep multi-unit prep timing and analytics, look at ConnectSmart instead.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
