Loss prevention

Delaget

QSR loss prevention plus back-office analytics; flags exceptions.

est. ~$300–600/mo/loc

Visit Delaget website →

What it costs

Quote-only (now part of PAR Technology, branded PAR OPS Detect); independent estimates run roughly $300-$600/mo per location. Often sold as part of a broader back-office suite.

Best for

Franchise and multi-unit QSR operators wanting loss prevention plus back-office analytics across many stores.

Pros

  • Ticket-level transaction detail with configurable loss alerts
  • Searches across all stores and systems in one place
  • Bundles loss prevention with back-office and AP automation
  • Proven at large scale across tens of thousands of locations

Cons

  • Built for franchise/multi-unit; overkill for one store
  • Pricing is quote-only and not transparent
  • Most value comes when bundled into the wider suite

Integrations

Integrates with QSR POS systems and PAR's back-office, payroll and food-cost tools.

Alternatives

Solink · DTiQ · Agilence

Our take: A capable multi-unit loss-prevention-plus-analytics platform for franchise operators; single independents will find it heavier and pricier than they need.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
