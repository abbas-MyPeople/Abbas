Food safety

Jolt

Digital checklists, temp logging, label printing and tasks.

~$80–200/mo/loc

Visit Jolt website →

What it costs

Quote-only; a single-location full bundle (checklists, time clock, scheduling, comms) was quoted around $296/mo plus a ~$549 one-time setup, often discounted. Multi-location runs roughly $150-$200/location/mo. Hardware (label printers, probes) sold separately.

Best for

Operators who want food safety plus daily operations (tasks, labels, scheduling) in one app.

Pros

  • Combines food safety with broader daily operations and tasks
  • Label printing and temp probe hardware available
  • Volume and annual-billing discounts for multi-location
  • Flexible modules you can mix to fit your needs

Cons

  • Pricing is quote-only with a setup fee
  • Manual temps unless you add sensor hardware
  • Module-based pricing can stack up

Integrations

Integrates with label printers and temperature probes/sensors; broad ops feature set beyond food safety.

Alternatives

FreshCheq · Squadle · ComplianceMate

Our take: A solid all-in-one for operators who want food safety and daily ops in a single app; just budget for setup fees and any sensor hardware.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
