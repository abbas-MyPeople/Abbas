POS

SpotOn

Restaurant POS plus marketing, loyalty, reviews and strong support.

$0–135/mo + payments

Visit SpotOn website →

What it costs

Two paths: a $0/mo All-In plan (~2.79% + 20¢ card-present, 2-year minimum term) or POS Essentials at ~$55/mo per station (lower ~2.45% + 15¢ processing, month-to-month), plus ~$3/mo per employee and a ~$75/mo minimum. Switching processors triggers a $995 conversion fee and doubles the software license cost.

Best for

Independents who want POS bundled with guest marketing, loyalty, and reviews — and value strong human support.

Pros

  • POS bundled with marketing, loyalty, and reviews
  • Strong, well-reviewed customer support
  • Month-to-month option on Essentials
  • Competitive processing rates on the paid plan

Cons

  • Must use SpotOn hardware and processing
  • All-In plan carries a 2-year minimum term
  • Steep penalties if you switch processors later
  • Per-employee and minimum fees nudge cost up

Integrations

Native SpotOn marketing, loyalty, reservations, and online ordering; integrates with common accounting and delivery tools, though the ecosystem is smaller than Toast's.

Alternatives

Toast · Square for Restaurants · Clover

Our take: A solid value when you want POS plus guest-marketing in one with real support — just go in clear-eyed about the contract terms and processor lock-in.
