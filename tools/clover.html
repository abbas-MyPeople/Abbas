POS

Clover

Flexible POS hardware and app marketplace, sold via banks/resellers.

~$15–90/mo/device + processing

Visit Clover website →

What it costs

Sold via banks and resellers, so pricing varies widely: software roughly $14–$90/mo per device, with restaurant plans cited around $135/mo (quick-service) to $179/mo (full-service), and hardware from ~$1,699 upfront. Card-present processing typically ~2.3–3.5% + 10¢, and most plans carry 36-month contracts with early-termination fees.

Best for

Operators wanting flexible, customizable POS hardware with a big app store, who are comfortable shopping resellers for a good rate.

Pros

  • Flexible hardware lineup and large app marketplace
  • Affordable entry software tiers
  • Widely supported and easy to source
  • Customizable to many service styles

Cons

  • Reseller pricing and rates vary wildly — easy to overpay
  • Common 36-month contracts with steep early-termination fees
  • Processing rates depend on your merchant account
  • Less restaurant-native than Toast or TouchBistro

Integrations

Large Clover app marketplace; integrates with accounting, online ordering, loyalty, and delivery tools — depth depends on apps you add.

Alternatives

Square for Restaurants · Toast · SpotOn

Our take: Flexible and affordable on paper, but the value hinges entirely on the reseller deal and contract — read the fine print and compare processing rates carefully.
