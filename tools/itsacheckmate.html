Delivery & aggregators

ItsaCheckmate

Connects 30+ delivery/ordering channels to your POS.

~$85–100/mo/loc

Visit ItsaCheckmate website →

What it costs

$85/mo per location for up to 2 delivery services, or $100/mo per location for unlimited services. No setup fees; month-to-month with 24/7 support.

Best for

Multi-channel operators wanting one menu pushed everywhere with orders flowing straight into the POS.

Pros

  • Transparent, simple per-location pricing
  • No setup fee and month-to-month, low-commitment
  • Connects 30+ delivery/ordering channels to the POS
  • Cited average savings of ~$247/mo versus manual entry

Cons

  • Only pays back at meaningful delivery volume
  • Unlimited-channel tier costs more
  • Focused on aggregation rather than broader marketing tools

Integrations

Connects 30+ delivery and ordering platforms into major POS systems (including Heartland Restaurant).

Alternatives

Otter · Deliverect · Chowly

Our take: One of the clearest, most transparently priced ways to push one menu everywhere and stop re-keying orders; great value once delivery volume is real.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
