Marketing

Attentive

Enterprise SMS (and email) marketing at scale.

$300+/mo + per-SMS

Visit Attentive website →

What it costs

Quote-only. Typically ~$300/mo base plus ~$0.01/SMS and carrier fees, with quarterly minimums of roughly $2,000-$3,000 (about $667-$1,000/mo before you send anything). A dedicated short code adds ~$500/mo. Contracts usually run 6-12 months; reported annual deals range widely (median around $40k).

Best for

High-volume, multi-location brands where SMS already drives a meaningful share of revenue.

Pros

  • Enterprise-grade SMS deliverability and list growth tools
  • Strong AI-assisted campaign and audience features
  • Scales to very large subscriber bases
  • Adds email alongside SMS

Cons

  • High minimums and multi-month contracts
  • Opaque, sales-led pricing
  • Too costly for a single independent location

Integrations

Integrates with major ecommerce and marketing stacks; restaurant fit depends on your POS/ordering connections.

Alternatives

Klaviyo · SlickText · Marsello

Our take: Powerful SMS at scale, but the minimums and contracts make it a multi-unit play. A single family restaurant will get more value from Klaviyo or SlickText.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
