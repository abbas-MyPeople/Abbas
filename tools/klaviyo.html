Klaviyo

Unified email + SMS with deep segmentation and POS integrations.

free–$150+/mo

Visit Klaviyo website →

What it costs

Free tier (up to 250 profiles, 500 emails/mo). Email plans scale by active profiles: ~$20/mo at 500 contacts, ~$100/mo at 5,000, ~$150/mo at 10,000, ~$720/mo at 50,000. SMS is a separate add-on subscription. Note: since Feb 2025 billing is based on all active profiles, not just contacts you email.

Best for

Independents and small groups that want serious email + SMS automation (win-backs, segmentation) without an enterprise contract.

Pros

  • Genuinely free to start and grow into
  • Best-in-class segmentation and automated flows (win-back, birthday, lapsed)
  • Combined email + SMS with strong reporting
  • Wide POS/ecommerce integrations

Cons

  • Active-profile billing means a big list costs you even when you don't email everyone
  • SMS is billed as a separate subscription plus per-message credits
  • Built for ecommerce first; restaurant features are not native

Integrations

Connects to many POS and online-ordering/ecommerce platforms; Shopify and Square among the most common. SMS, email, and WhatsApp run under the platform.

Alternatives

Attentive · Marsello · SlickText

Our take: The most capable email + SMS engine you can start free, but watch the active-profile billing as your list grows. Great for operators willing to learn it; overkill if you just want simple texts.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
