Loman.ai

AI phone agent that takes orders, payment and reservations.

from $199/mo

Visit Loman.ai website →

What it costs

Monthly subscription from ~$199/mo; reported tiers include a Standard plan around $299/mo (up to ~500 minutes, one location) and Premium around $529/mo (up to ~1,000 minutes, up to 3 locations, voice cloning). Custom-quoted by team size and needs; one of the lower-priced order-taking agents.

Best for

Independents and small groups that want the phone to actually take orders, reservations, and payment around the clock.

Pros

  • Takes phone orders, reservations, and payment
  • Lower-priced than most order-taking AI agents
  • Handles unlimited simultaneous calls
  • Built-in upsell engine to lift ticket size

Cons

  • Base plans have minute caps
  • Younger vendor with a shorter track record
  • POS integration depth varies (falls back to fax/email)
  • Custom pricing requires a quote

Integrations

Syncs orders/reservations to supported POS systems where available, with fax/email fallback when direct integration isn't possible.

Alternatives

Slang.ai · Newo.ai · Goodcall

Our take: One of the most affordable ways to capture phone orders 24/7 — a strong pick for order-heavy independents, just confirm POS integration for your system.
