Food safety

SmartSense by Digi

Wire-free temp sensors plus HACCP compliance and alerts.

est. ~$100–300/mo/loc

Visit SmartSense by Digi website →

What it costs

Sensing-as-a-Service: hardware/install plus subscription, sales-led with no public self-serve pricing (one institutional quote showed ~$34k install plus ~$4,250/year monitoring; restaurant pricing varies widely, est. ~$100-$300/mo per location).

Best for

Multi-unit and enterprise operators needing rigorous, audit-ready temperature monitoring across the food flow.

Pros

  • Monitors temperature across receiving, storage, prep and service
  • Built for HACCP/FDA/USDA compliance and audit trails
  • Enterprise-grade reliability and reporting
  • Backed by Digi's hardware and connectivity

Cons

  • Sales-led with no self-serve pricing
  • Hardware and install costs can be substantial
  • Aimed at larger operators, not single shops

Integrations

Wireless sensor network plus cloud platform; Sensing-as-a-Service model spanning the full food flow.

Alternatives

ComplianceMate · Therma · Squadle

Our take: Enterprise-grade monitoring and audit trails for serious multi-unit operators; the install cost and sales process make it heavy for an independent restaurant.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
