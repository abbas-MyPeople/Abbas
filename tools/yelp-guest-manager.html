Reservations

Yelp Guest Manager

Reservations and waitlist across Yelp/Google/Apple.

~$199–299/mo, no cover fees

Visit Yelp Guest Manager website →

What it costs

Three editions: Basic ~$99/mo (up to 500 covers/mo), Plus ~$299/mo (unlimited covers, POS integration, custom floor plan), and Enterprise (custom); no per-cover fees. New customers can get up to 60 days free plus Yelp Ads credit.

Best for

Fast-casual and full-service venues wanting reservations plus waitlist with wide discovery and no per-cover fees.

Pros

  • No per-cover fees
  • Discovery across Yelp, Google, and Apple
  • Waitlist, two-way texting, and to-go management included
  • Generous intro offers and ads credit

Cons

  • Tied to the Yelp ecosystem
  • POS integration and floor plans gated to Plus tier
  • Discovery network skews to Yelp users
  • Less prestige than OpenTable/Resy for fine dining

Integrations

POS integration on the Plus plan; reservations and waitlist surface across Yelp, Google, and Apple Maps.

Alternatives

OpenTable · Resy · Waitwhile

Our take: A solid, fee-light reservations and waitlist option with real discovery reach — especially attractive if your guests already find you on Yelp.
