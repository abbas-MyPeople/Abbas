Energy & maintenance

Verdigris

Circuit-level energy monitoring with AI power/cooling optimization.

est. ~$200–500/mo/loc

Visit Verdigris website →

What it costs

Panel-installed meters plus cloud subscription; older public references cited ~$99-$225/mo, but the company now skews toward larger sites/data centers, so expect a custom quote (independent estimates ~$200-$500/mo per location).

Best for

Energy-intensive single sites or portfolios wanting circuit-level visibility into exactly where power is wasted.

Pros

  • Circuit-level monitoring pinpoints where power and money leak
  • AI optimizes HVAC and cooling automatically
  • Fast ROI claimed through 20%+ energy cost cuts
  • Detects power-quality issues and equipment anomalies

Cons

  • Requires panel-level hardware install by an electrician
  • Increasingly focused on data centers and large portfolios
  • Pricing is now largely quote-based

Integrations

EV2 panel meters feed cloud analytics; integrates with building management for adaptive HVAC automation.

Alternatives

GridPoint · Powerhouse Dynamics Open Kitchen · Ecotrak

Our take: Unmatched circuit-level detail on where your power goes, but the panel install and enterprise focus make it a fit mainly for energy-heavy or multi-site operators.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
