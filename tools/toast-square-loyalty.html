Loyalty & CRM

Toast / Square Loyalty

Simple points/visits loyalty bundled with your POS.

$45–185/mo

Visit Toast / Square Loyalty website →

What it costs

Square Loyalty runs ~$45/mo as a POS add-on (Square Marketing/email is a further ~$15/mo). Toast Loyalty is bundled into Toast's Marketing package at ~$185/mo on top of your Toast POS subscription, so all-in is often $250-$350+/mo.

Best for

Restaurants already on Toast or Square that want simple, no-fuss loyalty tied to the POS.

Pros

  • Native to your POS, so setup and tracking are easy
  • Square's add-on is genuinely cheap to start
  • Points/visits loyalty that drives repeat visits
  • One vendor, one bill

Cons

  • Basic feature set; no real AI personalization
  • Data is locked to that POS, and switching loses it
  • Toast's loyalty is bundled into a pricier marketing package

Integrations

Tightly integrated with Toast POS or Square POS respectively; limited use outside that ecosystem.

Alternatives

TapMango · Marsello · Como

Our take: The path of least resistance if you're already on Toast or Square. It's basic, but for a small shop that just wants a points program, that's often enough.
