Loss prevention

Solink

Pairs camera video with POS data to review suspect transactions.

from ~$175/mo/loc

Visit Solink website →

What it costs

Custom/quote-based subscription, commonly cited from around $175/mo per location, scaling with cameras and package. Works with your existing compatible cameras.

Best for

Single and multi-location operators who want to tie security video to POS transactions to catch theft and voids.

Pros

  • Pairs video with the exact receipt/transaction for fast review
  • Works with existing compatible cameras and many POS systems
  • Cloud access to footage and alerts from anywhere
  • Useful for both loss prevention and operational coaching

Cons

  • Needs compatible cameras; may require hardware
  • Pricing is quote-based, not published per location
  • Real value requires actually reviewing the flagged events

Integrations

Integrates POS data, access control and business intelligence with video; broad POS compatibility.

Alternatives

DTiQ · Envysion · Delaget

Our take: The most accessible video-plus-POS loss prevention tool for independents, catching theft you'd otherwise miss, provided your cameras are compatible.
