Emerging AI

Voosh

AI review responder plus delivery-app dispute and recovery.

est. ~$ quote

Visit Voosh website →

What it costs

Quote-based, varying by number of locations; vendor claims ROI typically 4-5x the cost. Sweet spot is mid-market brands of ~20-30 locations, though it serves single sites too.

Best for

Delivery-heavy operators wanting to automate third-party disputes, finances and review responses in one place.

Pros

  • AI dispute management with a high reported win rate (~80-90%)
  • Recovers disputed delivery funds and reconciles payouts
  • AI review responses across delivery apps and local search
  • One screen for finances, disputes, promotions and downtime

Cons

  • More a delivery-ops tool than a guest-facing chatbot
  • Pricing is quote-only
  • Best value concentrated in mid-market multi-unit brands

Integrations

Connects to DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub and other delivery platforms plus review sources.

Alternatives

Loop AI · Otter · Birdeye (reviews)

Our take: A strong delivery-ops recovery and review tool for off-premise-heavy brands; just understand it's about disputes and finances, not guest chat.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
