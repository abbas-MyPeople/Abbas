Deliverect

Order injection from delivery apps into POS, menu sync, dispatch.

~$99+/mo/loc

Visit Deliverect website →

What it costs

Custom/quote-based, scaling with locations, digital volume and modules (Restaurants, Retail, Direct, Dispatch). Independent estimates start around $99+/mo per location; no free plan.

Best for

Delivery-heavy and multi-location operators wanting robust order injection, menu sync and dispatch across channels.

Pros

  • Strong multi-channel order injection and menu sync
  • Scales from a handful to thousands of locations
  • Adds dispatch and direct-ordering options
  • Mature platform with wide channel coverage

Cons

  • Custom pricing; add-on modules raise true cost
  • No free plan and no fully public pricing
  • More than a low-volume single shop needs

Integrations

Injects delivery-app orders into POS, syncs menus and supports dispatch; broad POS and channel coverage.

Alternatives

Otter · ItsaCheckmate · Chowly

Our take: A robust, scalable middleware for delivery-heavy operators; just watch how add-on modules stack up the real monthly cost.
