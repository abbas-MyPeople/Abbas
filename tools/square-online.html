Online ordering

Square Online

Free site builder with pickup/delivery ordering on Square.

$0–149/mo

Visit Square Online website →

What it costs

Free site builder ($0/mo) with pickup/delivery ordering on Square; paid plans (roughly $29–$79+/mo) remove ads and add features, and you pay standard Square processing per order. No commission on direct orders.

Best for

Square users and budget-conscious independents who want a zero-cost way to launch commission-free online ordering.

Pros

  • Free to start with no commission on direct orders
  • Seamless if you already run Square POS
  • Quick to set up; syncs menu and payments automatically
  • Pickup, delivery, and curbside built in

Cons

  • Generic commerce tool, lighter on restaurant-specific upsell
  • Locked to Square processing
  • Fewer marketing/brand features than BentoBox or Owner
  • Design flexibility is limited

Integrations

Native to Square POS, Loyalty, and Payments; on-demand delivery via Square's delivery partners.

Alternatives

ChowNow · BentoBox · Owner.com

Our take: The cheapest credible path to commission-free ordering, especially for existing Square users — just don't expect the brand polish of a premium restaurant platform.
