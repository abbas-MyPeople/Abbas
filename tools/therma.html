Therma

Wireless temp/humidity sensors with 24/7 spoilage alerts.

from $10/mo/sensor

Visit Therma website →

What it costs

From $10/mo per sensor, no minimum order. Buy only the sensors you need; hardware, 4G connectivity and alerts included.

Best for

Any restaurant or ghost kitchen wanting cheap 24/7 temperature monitoring on coolers and freezers.

Pros

  • Very low per-sensor cost with no minimum order
  • 24/7 wireless monitoring with SMS, dashboard and app alerts
  • Long-range LoRa sensors reach through walls and freezers
  • One avoided walk-in failure can pay for years of service

Cons

  • Monitoring only; not a full HACCP checklist workflow
  • You add sensors per fridge/freezer, so cost scales with units
  • Less of an all-in-one ops tool than rivals

Integrations

Standalone sensor network with app/dashboard alerts; focused on temperature and humidity monitoring.

Alternatives

ComplianceMate · SmartSense by Digi · Squadle

Our take: The simplest, cheapest insurance against a spoiled walk-in; pair it with a checklist tool if you also need full HACCP logging.
