Forecasting

Lineup.ai

AI sales/labor/item forecasts from weather, events, history.

~$149/mo/loc

Visit Lineup.ai website →

What it costs

Standard plan around $149/mo per location, with lower entry pricing reportedly from ~$79/mo. Free trial with no credit card required; per-location subscription model.

Best for

QSR, fast-casual, and full-service operators who want AI sales and labor forecasts to staff and prep to real demand.

Pros

  • AI forecasts of sales, labor, and item-level demand
  • Factors in weather, events, and history
  • Affordable per-location pricing with a free trial
  • Real-time dashboards and variance analytics

Cons

  • Forecasting-focused; not a full inventory or scheduling suite
  • Needs roughly six months of data for best accuracy
  • Value depends on acting on the forecasts

Integrations

Connects to POS systems and labor-scheduling tools to pull history and push forecasts.

Alternatives

5-Out · Tenzo · 7shifts

Our take: An affordable, restaurant-specific forecasting tool that helps you stop staffing and prepping by gut. Worth it once you have the history to feed it and a plan to act on the numbers.
