Online ordering

ChowNow

Commission-free branded online ordering, app and marketing.

$119–328/mo

Visit ChowNow website →

What it costs

Commission-free model: subscription roughly $149–$199/mo per location plus standard ~2.95% + 29¢ processing, with a one-time setup fee (often $119–$499). Month-to-month, annual, and 2-year contract options; delivery via Flex is a flat per-order fee.

Best for

Independents who want a branded, commission-free ordering channel to escape the 15–30% delivery-app cut.

Pros

  • 0% commission on direct orders — keeps your margin
  • Branded ordering page plus a marketplace app
  • Marketing tools to drive repeat orders
  • Flexible contract terms available

Cons

  • Subscription owed even in slow months
  • Processing and setup fees on top of the monthly
  • Less of a full website/brand builder than BentoBox
  • Pays off best at steady direct-order volume

Integrations

Integrates with major POS systems (Toast, Square, Clover, and others) and pushes orders into the kitchen; connects to delivery dispatch.

Alternatives

Owner.com · Square Online · BentoBox

Our take: A proven, restaurant-focused way to own commission-free ordering — the flat fee is well worth it once you're driving meaningful direct volume.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
