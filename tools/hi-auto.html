What it costs

Pricing not public — quote only; deployed across ~1,000 stores for QSR chains. (Existing estimate ~$500–1,500/mo/loc is illustrative only — confirm on a live quote; comparable rivals advertise around $475/mo.)

Best for

QSR chains wanting accurate, high-throughput AI order-taking in the drive-thru and on the phone.

Pros

  • High accuracy (~96%) and order completion (~93%+) at scale
  • Purpose-built for noisy drive-thru environments
  • Bilingual English/Spanish support
  • Optimized upselling and labor efficiency

Cons

  • Quote-only pricing
  • Chain-focused; limited fit for full-service or single sites
  • Requires POS/drive-thru hardware integration
  • Rollout effort across locations

Integrations

POS-integrated drive-thru and phone ordering; designed for franchise/multi-unit deployment with location-level flexibility.

Alternatives

SoundHound Dynamic Drive-Thru · ConverseNow · Tillster

Our take: A drive-thru voice specialist with strong accuracy at scale — a good chain choice, but the quote-only enterprise model isn't built for a single independent.
