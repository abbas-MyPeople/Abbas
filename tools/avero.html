Analytics & BI

Avero

Sales, labor and server-performance analytics and benchmarking.

est. ~$300–1,200/mo

Visit Avero website →

What it costs

Starter free; Essentials from ~$300/location/year; Professional from ~$600/location/year. Implementation, integrations and training can add meaningful one-time cost.

Best for

Full-service restaurants and groups wanting deep sales, labor and server-performance analytics with benchmarking.

Pros

  • Deep operational analytics for sales, labor and server performance
  • Benchmarking to compare locations and staff
  • Free Starter tier to test the waters
  • Restaurant-specific reporting, not generic BI

Cons

  • Implementation and integration costs add up
  • Strongest value at multi-unit/full-service scale
  • More than a small QSR typically needs

Integrations

Connects to POS and labor systems to pull sales, labor and server-level data for analysis.

Alternatives

Tenzo (BI) · Mirus · Restaurant365

Our take: Powerful operational analytics for full-service groups, with a free tier to start; the real value (and cost) shows up at multi-unit scale.
