Emerging AI

PreciTaste

AI + computer vision for kitchen task and production management.

est. ~$1,000+/mo/loc

Visit PreciTaste website →

What it costs

Quote-only, enterprise/chain-oriented; no public pricing (independent estimates ~$1,000+/mo per location). Vision-AI deployment with cameras and sensors.

Best for

QSR and fast-casual chains wanting AI to predict prep and manage production so the line stays ahead of demand.

Pros

  • Predicts prep and production to keep the line ahead of demand
  • Claimed ~7% lower food cost, ~50% less waste and labor savings
  • Live inventory sensing via computer vision
  • Proven at scale (Chipotle and others, 2,500+ systems)

Cons

  • Enterprise/chain focus; complex back-of-house integration
  • No public pricing; high estimated cost per location
  • Requires cameras and sensors installed in the kitchen

Integrations

Vision-AI platform with Prep, Planner and Station Assistants; integrates with kitchen equipment and inventory.

Alternatives

Agot AI · Lineup.ai · 5-Out

Our take: Powerful demand-driven prep and waste reduction proven at major chains; the install complexity and enterprise pricing make it unrealistic for independents.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
