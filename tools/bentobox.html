Online ordering

BentoBox

Premium restaurant website/CMS plus commission-free ordering & catering.

~$149–479/mo

Visit BentoBox website →

What it costs

Website plans from ~$119/mo, with popular packages around $279/mo (Foundations) and $479/mo (Signature); Takeout & Delivery adds ~$49/mo plus ~$0.99/order and 3% processing, and Online Catering ~$79/mo. Month-to-month; total cost scales with order volume.

Best for

Full-service and fine-dining brands that want the best-looking restaurant website with ordering and catering built in.

Pros

  • Premium, design-forward restaurant websites and CMS
  • Commission-free ordering and strong catering tools
  • Brand and content control, not just a menu page
  • No long-term contract

Cons

  • Per-order and processing fees stack on top of subscription
  • Strength is website/brand, not high-volume ordering
  • Total cost climbs quickly at higher order counts
  • Pricier than lean ordering-only tools

Integrations

Integrates with major POS and reservation systems; first-party ordering, catering, events, and email marketing.

Alternatives

Owner.com · ChowNow · Popmenu

Our take: The pick when your website and brand are the priority and ordering is secondary — beautiful and capable, but watch the per-order fees as volume grows.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
