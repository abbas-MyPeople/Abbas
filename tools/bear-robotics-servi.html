Bear Robotics Servi

Autonomous food-running and bussing robot.

$11,990 or ~$293/mo

Visit Bear Robotics Servi website →

What it costs

Buy outright around $11,990 (Servi) or ~$16,000 (Servi+), or lease via RaaS from roughly $293-$1,500/mo depending on model and term (12/24/36 months). Operating cost often cited near $15/day.

Best for

Full-service and fast-casual rooms with open layouts that want to take running and bussing off servers.

Pros

  • Affordable entry compared with cooking robots; clear buy-or-lease options
  • Cuts steps for servers so they spend more time with guests
  • Low daily operating cost versus an extra hire
  • Lease includes software updates, warranty and analytics

Cons

  • Runs and busses food but doesn't cook anything
  • Needs wide aisles and a fairly open floor plan to navigate
  • Novelty can wear off; not a fix for kitchen bottlenecks

Integrations

Largely standalone; calls and routing managed through Bear's app and floor mapping rather than deep POS ties.

Alternatives

Pudu Robotics · Keenon Robotics · Richtech Robotics

Our take: The most realistic robot for an independent full-service spot, easing server workload at a fair price, as long as your dining room layout suits it.
