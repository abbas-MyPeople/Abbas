Homebase

Scheduling, time tracking, payroll and hiring for SMBs.

free–$99.95/mo/loc

Visit Homebase website →

What it costs

Free plan for one location (basic scheduling and time tracking). Paid per location/month: Essentials ~$24.95, Plus ~$59.95, All-in-One ~$99.95 (all unlimited employees). Payroll add-on ~$39/mo base plus $6/active employee.

Best for

Single-location small businesses that want free scheduling and time tracking plus optional payroll and hiring.

Pros

  • Free for one location, covering the basics well
  • Per-location pricing with unlimited employees (no per-head fee on core plans)
  • Adds time tracking, hiring, and payroll as you grow
  • Easy for non-technical owners

Cons

  • Per-location pricing jumps the moment you add a second site
  • Less restaurant-specific forecasting than 7shifts
  • Best HR/compliance features only on the top tier

Integrations

Integrates with common POS systems and payroll providers; broad small-business tool support.

Alternatives

7shifts · Sling · Deputy

Our take: Hard to beat for a single location thanks to the free tier and unlimited employees. If you run multiple sites, the per-location cost adds up fast, so compare with Deputy or Sling.
