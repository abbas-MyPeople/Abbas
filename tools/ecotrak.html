Ecotrak

Facility/asset CMMS: work orders, preventive maintenance, vendors.

from $25/mo/loc

Visit Ecotrak website →

What it costs

Ecotrak Build (self-service CMMS) at $25/mo per location with a 30-day free trial; enterprise tiers quoted separately.

Best for

QSRs, franchisees and small operators who want a cheap way to track assets, work orders and preventive maintenance.

Pros

  • Very low entry price for a self-service CMMS
  • Tracks assets, work orders, preventive maintenance and vendors
  • Helps avoid surprise equipment failures and repair costs
  • Free trial and quick setup for small businesses

Cons

  • Maintenance management, not real-time energy metering
  • Per-location fee scales across many sites
  • Lighter analytics than enterprise energy platforms

Integrations

Asset/facility platform with vendor management; connects equipment records and service workflows.

Alternatives

UpKeep · Powerhouse Dynamics Open Kitchen · ServiceChannel

Our take: An affordable, practical way to stop surprise repair bills and keep equipment records straight; just know it's maintenance tracking, not live energy monitoring.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
