Tenzo (BI)

Unifies sales/labor/inventory/reviews into one dashboard.

from ~$600/yr

Visit Tenzo (BI) website →

What it costs

From around $600/year; scales by modules and locations. Free trial available.

Best for

Fast-casual, full-service and ghost-kitchen operators wanting sales, labor, inventory and reviews in one dashboard.

Pros

  • Unifies POS, labor, inventory and review data in one view
  • AI-powered analytics and sales forecasting
  • Dashboards for both GMs and head office
  • Free trial and relatively quick time-to-value

Cons

  • Per-module and per-location pricing climbs as you add
  • Analytics-led; not an operations or accounting system
  • Best value at multi-location scale

Integrations

Aggregates data from POS, scheduling/labor, inventory and review platforms, plus weather and events.

Alternatives

Avero · Mirus · Restaurant365

Our take: A genuinely useful single pane of glass across your operation that starts affordably; just watch how per-module, per-location pricing adds up as you grow.
