Visit Revel Systems website →

What it costs

iPad POS from ~$99/terminal/mo (billed annually on a 3-year Revel Advantage contract, 2-terminal minimum), with implementation packages from ~$674 and ~2.49% processing via Revel Advantage. Contracts often auto-renew and early-termination fees can run into the thousands.

Best for

High-volume and multi-unit QSR or chains that need a robust POS built for throughput.

Pros

  • Built for high volume and many locations
  • Strong inventory, CRM, and reporting in the base
  • Scales across multi-unit operations
  • Mature, established platform

Cons

  • 3-year contract plus 2-terminal minimum
  • High setup/implementation fees
  • Auto-renewal and large early-termination fees
  • Overkill and over-budget for a single small shop

Integrations

Open API plus partner integrations for accounting, online ordering, loyalty, and delivery; tied to Revel Advantage processing for best pricing.

Alternatives

Toast · Lightspeed Restaurant · Square for Restaurants

Our take: A capable POS for volume and scale, but the 3-year contract, minimums, and setup costs make it a poor fit for independents — best reserved for growing chains.
