Tenzo

Analytics plus demand forecasting across locations.

from ~$600/yr

Visit Tenzo website →

What it costs

Starts around $600/year; priced per module and per location, so cost climbs with scale. Most operators recoup it by catching one area of waste; typically live within two weeks.

Best for

Multi-site fast-casual and full-service operators who want analytics plus demand forecasting in one dashboard.

Pros

  • Machine-learning forecasts up to three weeks out, more accurate than rolling averages
  • Unifies sales, labor, inventory, and reviews into one view
  • Pulls in weather, holidays, and local events
  • Quick setup and low entry price

Cons

  • Needs at least ~90 days of history to forecast
  • Per-module, per-location pricing climbs
  • Analytics-led; not a scheduling or inventory system itself

Integrations

Aggregates data from POS, labor planners, inventory tools, review platforms, and external weather/events feeds.

Alternatives

Lineup.ai · 5-Out · Avero

Our take: A strong one-pane-of-glass for operators who want forecasting and analytics together. The pricing grows with modules and sites, so scope what you actually need.
