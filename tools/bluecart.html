Inventory & purchasing

BlueCart

B2B wholesale ordering/procurement plus inventory.

$10–200/mo

Visit BlueCart website →

What it costs

Very low entry: listing/access plans cited around $10/mo, with a higher tier around $200/mo, plus custom plans. Pricing is not fully transparent for the buyer side; confirm scope on a quote.

Best for

Operators who mainly want to streamline supplier ordering and procurement at a low entry price.

Pros

  • Low cost to get started
  • Centralizes ordering across multiple suppliers in one place
  • Order, delivery, and invoice matching automated
  • Adds inventory plus newer predictive ordering and menu-profitability tools

Cons

  • Procurement-first; lighter on deep recipe/food-cost analysis
  • Pricing scope not fully transparent
  • Best value depends on your suppliers being on the platform

Integrations

Connects buyers and food-and-beverage distributors on one platform; integrates ordering, inventory, and invoicing.

Alternatives

Notch · MarketMan · MarginEdge

Our take: A cheap, practical way to consolidate supplier ordering, especially if your vendors already use it. If costing depth is the goal, pair it with MarketMan or MarginEdge.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
