What it costs

Quote-only with one flat monthly fee covering unlimited transactions and approvers; independent estimates start around $200/mo. Contact sales for a tailored quote.

Best for

Restaurants drowning in supplier invoices that want AP coding, approvals and payments automated.

Pros

  • AI reads and codes invoices, cutting manual data entry
  • Flat fee with unlimited transactions and approvers
  • Streamlines approvals and vendor payments in one flow
  • Restaurant/hospitality roots with broad accounting integrations

Cons

  • AP-only; you still need accounting and payroll elsewhere
  • Pricing is quote-only, so budgeting needs a sales call
  • Lower value if your invoice volume is small

Alternatives

MarginEdge · Restaurant365 · BILL
