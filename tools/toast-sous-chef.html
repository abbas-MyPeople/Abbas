What it costs

Bundled within Toast (now part of the Toast IQ intelligence ecosystem); no separate standalone price.

Best for

Toast restaurants that want plain-language answers and proactive insights from their own sales and labor data.

Pros

  • Turns Toast data into plain-language answers and recommendations
  • Proactively surfaces insights via a personalized feed
  • Can take actions like updating menus or editing shifts in Toast
  • Menu upsell features have lifted average order volume in trials

Cons

  • Toast-only; useless outside the Toast ecosystem
  • An assistant, not an autonomous agent
  • Now folded into the broader Toast IQ branding

Integrations

Native to Toast POS and the Toast IQ ecosystem; works across menus, scheduling and reporting within Toast.

Alternatives

Tenzo (BI) · Avero · Restaurant365 analytics

Our take: A handy, increasingly capable AI layer if you already run Toast; there's no reason to consider it otherwise.
