Toast

All-in-one restaurant OS: POS, ordering, KDS, payroll.

from $69/mo + hardware

Visit Toast website →

What it costs

Tiered: a free Starter Kit (with higher ~3.09% + 15¢ in-person processing), a Point of Sale plan at $69/mo (~2.49% + 15¢), and Build Your Own from ~$110/mo; proprietary hardware runs $799+ per terminal, and online orders process at ~3.50% + 15¢. Real-world single-location cost is usually $150–$500/mo in software plus processing, and add-ons like online ordering, loyalty, and KDS run $50–$165/mo each.

Best for

Operators who want one connected restaurant OS instead of a stack of disconnected tools, and don't mind committing to Toast's hardware and processing.

Pros

  • All-in-one: POS, online ordering, KDS, payroll, loyalty under one roof
  • Deep restaurant-specific features and a huge integration ecosystem
  • Android handhelds built for tableside ordering and speed
  • Capital/financing and 0% hardware financing options ease upfront cost

Cons

  • Proprietary hardware and processing lock-in
  • All-in monthly cost often climbs past $1,000 once add-ons stack
  • Add-on pricing and onboarding fees (~$95/hr) add up fast
  • Stuck on Toast's payment rates

Integrations

Massive native integration marketplace plus first-party online ordering, KDS, payroll, loyalty; most third-party delivery and back-office tools connect.

Alternatives

Square for Restaurants · SpotOn · TouchBistro

Our take: The most complete all-in-one restaurant platform, and a strong pick if you'll use the whole stack — just budget honestly for hardware, processing, and add-ons, and accept the lock-in.
