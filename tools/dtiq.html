DTiQ

Managed video surveillance plus POS exception analytics.

est. ~$300–700/mo/loc

Visit DTiQ website →

What it costs

Quote-only subscription (no upfront capex); independent estimates around $300-$700/mo per location depending on cameras and managed-service level.

Best for

Operators who want outsourced video surveillance plus POS exception analytics with human-reviewed audits.

Pros

  • Managed service combines video, POS data and human SmartAudit reviews
  • Subscription model avoids big upfront hardware spend
  • Integrates with major POS platforms (Square, Toast, Revel)
  • Real-time insights for loss prevention and operations

Cons

  • Managed service plus hardware means higher ongoing cost
  • Typically contract-based
  • Pricing not published; requires a sales conversation

Integrations

Integrates with leading POS systems; combines its 360iQ, VIDEOiQ and AI tools with managed audit services.

Alternatives

Solink · Delaget · Envysion

Our take: Good for operators who'd rather outsource video review to experts than do it themselves, but the managed-service model carries a higher, contract-based cost.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
