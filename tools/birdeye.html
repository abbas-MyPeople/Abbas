Birdeye

Multi-site review generation/monitoring with AI replies.

$299–449/mo/loc

Visit Birdeye website →

What it costs

Quote-only with prepaid annual tiers: Starter ~$299/mo per location, Growth ~$399/mo, Dominate ~$499/mo. Monthly billing runs higher. Per-location cost drops with more locations; onboarding may apply.

Best for

Multi-location brands that need review generation, monitoring, and listings managed across many sites.

Pros

  • Built for managing reputation across many locations
  • Strong review generation, monitoring, and AI replies
  • Adds listings, surveys, social listening, and competitor monitoring at higher tiers
  • Well-rated enterprise platform with deep reporting

Cons

  • Per-location pricing plus onboarding is heavy for a single site
  • Initial setup complexity
  • Quote-based; no transparent self-serve pricing

Integrations

Integrates with Google, Yelp, Facebook and 200+ review/listing sites, plus many CRM and business systems.

Alternatives

Podium · Marqii · GatherUp

Our take: A robust, enterprise-leaning reputation platform that shines across multiple locations. For one independent restaurant it's likely more tool and cost than you need.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
