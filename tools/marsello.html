Marsello

Loyalty + email + SMS for hospitality, POS-connected.

from ~$60/mo/site

Visit Marsello website →

What it costs

Loyalty Launch around $60/mo and Loyalty Accelerate around $120/mo (prices quoted in NZD; SMS billed in USD), with an Enterprise tier. Marketing, SMS, and Social are paid add-ons that stack. Each extra location adds roughly $100-$200/mo depending on plan. 14-day free trial.

Best for

Hospitality and retail operators who want loyalty plus email/SMS marketing in one POS-connected tool.

Pros

  • Loyalty and marketing live together, so campaigns target real spend
  • Connects to common POS and ecommerce systems
  • Reasonable entry price for an all-in-one
  • No long contract; cancel anytime

Cons

  • Marketing and SMS are add-ons that raise the real monthly cost
  • Per-location pricing climbs for multi-site
  • Strongest as a loyalty tool; pure-marketing depth trails specialists

Integrations

Integrates with Lightspeed, Square, Shopify and other POS/ecommerce platforms; built for retail and hospitality.

Alternatives

Klaviyo · TapMango · SlickText

Our take: A tidy way to combine loyalty and marketing if you're already on a supported POS. Just price out the add-ons before you commit, because they stack.
