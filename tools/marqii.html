Marqii

Listings + reviews + menu sync across 50+ platforms.

$74.99–149.99/mo/loc

Visit Marqii website →

What it costs

Base ~$74.99/mo per location, Pro ~$119.99/mo, Pro+Advanced ~$149.99/mo, with custom pricing for 5+ locations. Optional done-for-you review-response bundles (AI + human) priced by review volume, up to ~500 responses/mo.

Best for

Operators who want listings, menus, and reviews kept accurate and answered across every platform from one dashboard.

Pros

  • Syncs menus and listings to Google, Yelp, Facebook, Apple Maps and 50+ platforms
  • POS menu changes update everywhere automatically
  • AI + human review responses available as a bundle
  • Transparent per-location pricing

Cons

  • Listings/menu-first; lighter on structured guest surveys
  • Review-response volume bundles add cost
  • Per-location pricing adds up for many sites

Integrations

Direct POS and online-ordering integrations plus a Yext partnership for listings; pushes to Google, Yelp, Facebook, Apple Maps and more.

Alternatives

Birdeye · GatherUp · Ovation

Our take: The best pick if your real pain is wrong hours and stale menus across the web as much as reviews. It keeps your digital presence consistent, but pair it with a survey tool if guest feedback is the priority.
