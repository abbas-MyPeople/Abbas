Loyalty & CRM

Thanx

Loyalty + CRM + agentic AI marketing for chains.

est. ~$ enterprise (3-yr)

Visit Thanx website →

What it costs

Quote-only, subscription/quotation-based; typically multi-year (often 3-year) commitments with extra fees for dedicated success management and support. No free plan.

Best for

Established restaurant chains (roughly 40+ locations) wanting agentic AI marketing and frictionless data capture.

Pros

  • Frictionless in-store data capture via credit-card linking
  • Agentic AI that runs personalized campaigns autonomously
  • Strong loyalty + CRM + digital experience in one
  • Restaurant-specific design and deep integrations

Cons

  • Long (often 3-year) contracts
  • Enterprise pricing; support tiers cost extra
  • Not aimed at single independents

Integrations

Integrates with Toast and other POS systems, web/app ordering, and via API into existing digital experiences.

Alternatives

Paytronix · Como · Incentivio

Our take: Powerful, modern loyalty for chains that want AI to run campaigns for them. The multi-year contract and enterprise focus rule it out for small operators.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
