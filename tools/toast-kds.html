Kitchen display (KDS)

Toast KDS

KDS routing POS/online/handheld orders to kitchen screens.

est. ~$ + hardware (Toast)

Visit Toast KDS website →

What it costs

Quote-only and tied to your Toast POS plan ($0-$69/mo, custom plans available; many restaurants run $165+/mo per terminal). Runs on Toast displays (~$799+) or iPad; KDS is bundled into higher Toast packages and priced via quote.

Best for

Restaurants already running Toast POS that want a deeply integrated kitchen display.

Pros

  • Seamless if you already use Toast POS
  • Routes POS, online, and handheld orders to kitchen screens
  • One vendor and one support line
  • Tight ticket flow across order channels

Cons

  • Locked to the Toast ecosystem
  • Opaque, quote-only pricing
  • Proprietary hardware adds upfront and replacement cost

Integrations

Native to Toast POS, online ordering, and handhelds; not designed to work outside Toast.

Alternatives

Fresh KDS · QSR Automations ConnectSmart · Square KDS

Our take: The obvious choice if you're committed to Toast, with the smoothest integration. If you're not on Toast, the lock-in and hardware cost make standalone options more sensible.

Pricing is the public list rate or a research estimate as of June 2026 — always confirm on a live quote. We take no commission from any vendor; this is for reference.
