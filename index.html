Startup • Social Platform

MyPeople

A revolutionary AI-powered social media platform that places genuine human interactions at its core. MyPeople enables users to connect precisely and instantly based on shared passions, goals, and aspirations. The AI precision matching engine finds exactly who you want to connect with, diving deeper into niches never possible before.

Why I Built This

I envisioned a platform that transforms digital human interactions. In a world where social media often creates superficial connections, MyPeople was born from the dream of making technology bring people genuinely closer — deeper and faster. Think of Aria, a student arriving alone in a new country, instantly finding locals who share her exact passions. That's the future we're building.

VedAstro

Contributing to a 9-year-old open-source Vedic astrology computation platform that democratizes complex astrological calculations. Inspired by the principle that ancient sage Parashara taught astrology freely, this project makes advanced predictive astrology accessible to everyone. It bridges the ancient wisdom of Vedic traditions with modern computation, featuring ML-trained models on 15,000+ birth charts for accuracy.

Philosopher & Visionary

I describe myself as a philosopher and a visionary with a deep inclination to learning — from fractal geometry to the Hindu concept of Brahman, from quantum mechanics to the cosmos. I believe the most profound truths lie at the intersection of science, spirituality, and human consciousness.

My curiosity knows no boundaries. Whether I'm exploring panpsychism (the idea that consciousness is fundamental to all matter), studying the interconnected web of life across spiritual traditions, or building technology that connects humans — I'm driven by the same question: How do we make deeper, more authentic connections?

A Writer's Mind

I write to make sense of the world. My Medium articles explore the landscapes where science meets spirituality, where modern culture collides with ancient wisdom. Topics range from the spectrum of consciousness and the harmonic universe to reflections on generational shifts and the complexities of human relationships.

Writing is how I process the cosmos-sized questions that keep me up at night. It's my way of contributing to the conversations that matter.
