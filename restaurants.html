Your questions, answered

Everything you'd want to ask before saying yes

I'm not technical at all. Is this really for me?

Especially for you. The whole point is that you don't have to understand the technology — that's what you're hiring me for. I explain everything in plain language, handle the setup, and leave you with tools that just work.

Will this replace my staff?

No — it removes the annoying parts so your team can focus on guests. The AI answers the phone during a rush, chases reviews, and handles repetitive admin. Your people do what people do best: hospitality.

How much does it cost?

It depends on what you actually need, and you'll see the full breakdown before committing: each tool's monthly cost, the estimated savings, and my fee — all on one transparent page. I'm a premium service, not the cheapest option, because the goal is for it to clearly pay for itself.

What if it doesn't work for my restaurant?

We start with a free audit and can begin with a single, low-risk win before expanding. I track real results and drop anything that isn't earning its keep. You're never locked into something that isn't working.

Do I have to switch my POS or systems?

Usually not. My specialty is integration — connecting AI tools to the systems you already use. If something genuinely needs to change, I'll tell you why and what it's worth, and only with your go-ahead.

Is my data and my customers' information safe?

Yes. Security is core to my enterprise work, and I bring that same standard here — using reputable platforms, proper access controls, and privacy-respecting setups. Your data stays yours.

How long does it take to get going?

A first win like the AI phone host can often be live quickly. Larger, multi-system integrations take longer. You'll get a realistic timeline in your transparent plan — no vague promises.

Why you, and not the software company directly?

Software vendors sell their one product and leave you to figure it out. I'm vendor-neutral: I choose the best tools for you, make them work together, prove them in my own restaurant first, and stay accountable for the outcome. One throat to choke, in the nicest way.

Do you only work with restaurants?

Restaurants are my focus and where I've proven it — but the same approach extends to retail, clinics, and service businesses. If you're nearby and curious, let's talk.

I have more than one location. Can you handle that?

Absolutely — multi-location is where good integration pays off most. Consistent systems, central reporting, and AI that scales across all your sites.
