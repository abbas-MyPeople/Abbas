How it works

You shouldn't need tech skills to run a modern restaurant.

Our philosophy is simple: the big chains win with technology and strategy you don't have time to learn. We bring it — the know-how, the industry playbook, and the team — and put it to work for you. You stay in control, you own your data, and we only get paid when you win. Here's exactly how it goes — tap any step:

1 You book a free call — we listen first

We get to know your restaurant and check that we're a fit. We'll show you the kinds of things we do for places like yours — but we won't prescribe anything until we've actually studied your business.

2 If it's a fit, we come walk your whole operation

We visit in person and walk your whole operation with you — not just where money is leaking, but where the right system could unlock time, sales, or a capability you don't have today. You decide what to share, and everything stays confidential. The more you show us, the more we can help.

3 You get a plan built around how your place actually runs — work and cost

You see precisely what we'll do and what each piece costs, in plain numbers — which parts are existing tools we'll set up and connect, and which parts we'll build just for you. You never have to touch the technology — we handle all of it. The only things we'll ask of you are the few that only you can authorize or access, and we'll walk you through those.

4 You start saving — then we get paid

With the right strategies you will save money and start putting money aside — that's our guarantee. How fast and how much, we estimate up front, but we won't promise a number we can't control. We take payment only after you've felt the benefit.

5 We prove it in your real numbers

For strategy work we track month-over-month sales straight from your POS and the delivery apps — no spreadsheets, nothing massaged. Setup is a clear one-time fee. And if we connect you to a service, our fee comes from you, never a commission from the vendor — so the only thing we ever recommend is what's right for your restaurant. Then we agree on simple ongoing pricing to keep it all running.

Good questions

What owners ask us first.

Is it just one person?

The person is the point — and there's a team behind him. You're led by Abbas, who's both an ex-Google software engineer and a hands-on restaurant owner-operator — a combination almost no vendor, agency, or consultant has. That one mind sets the standard, sees how every layer connects, and stays your point of contact, while a full team builds, integrates, tests, and runs it all to a production standard. You get the singular, cross-domain expertise and real horsepower — no single point of failure, and no 20 disconnected vendors.

What happens on the free call?

We talk through your restaurant — where it's winning and where it's leaking money — and point you to the highest-impact move, whether or not we end up working together. No slides, no pressure.

Do you disappear after it's built?

Never. We don't do drop-and-leave. We stay on — automated monitoring plus real check-ins — because the tools and the market keep evolving, and so should your restaurant. That long-term partnership is the whole point.

Can't I just do this myself?

You can — and the finder above points you straight to the tools. But choosing among dozens of options, wiring them into your POS, and proving the ROI is the hard, time-consuming part. That's exactly what we do, end to end — so you get the result without the risk.

What if no tool out there does what my restaurant needs?

Then we build it. That's the part almost no one else offers: our engineers write custom software around your workflow — the way we built our own restaurant's AI phone agent, its AI-readable website, and its living growth engine when nothing off the shelf was good enough. And when the best fix isn't software at all — a card on the table, a smarter offer, the right words from your server — we engineer that too. Whatever produces the result.

How are you different from a marketing agency or consultant?

An agency bills a retainer for advice and slides, and profits whether or not you do. We're operators who build and run the actual systems, take no vendor commissions, and get paid only once the savings show up in your real numbers — month to month, cancel anytime. We only win when you win.

Do you handle more than one location?

Yes. With 2–10 locations the biggest win is usually seeing across all of them — one clear view of sales, labor, and food cost in real time, so a store that's slipping shows up while you can still fix it, not at month-end. We roll out one location first, prove it, then repeat.

What is the AI-visibility growth engine?

It's how we keep your site getting found instead of going stale. Each week our living-website engine watches your data and the wider world — what's selling, what people search and ask AI assistants, what's changed — then proposes the specific site changes most likely to win more visibility and orders. It emails you a short list to approve, and automatically ships the ones you say yes to. Nothing goes live without your say-so. We proved it on our own restaurant, where it runs on a schedule and already sends the weekly approval email — it's the recurring growth layer included in the ongoing plans.
