The story

From shipping software at Google to running a kitchen in Spring, TX.

I started as a builder — an engineer who liked turning messy, complicated problems into things that just work. That took me to Google, where as a Solutions Engineer I helped partners ship technology to millions of users, and later to WSO2, one of the world's leading integration companies, where I'm now a Senior Lead Solutions Architect.

In that role I sit across the table from enterprises of every size — household names like Hard Rock Cafe, major universities, government agencies, and organizations in healthcare and the public sector. Across roughly 30 enterprise accounts, my job is the same: understand the business, cut through the hype, and connect the right systems so everything runs smoothly and securely.

But I'm not only an engineer. I've also founded and run my own businesses — including Wok & Karahi, a halal Chinese-Indian-Pakistani restaurant in Spring, Texas that locals genuinely love. So I know both sides: the technology, and the 7-day-a-week reality of running a small business on thin margins.

That combination is rare — and it's exactly why I started AZ Integrations. There's a flood of brilliant AI tools out there, but restaurant owners don't have the time or the technical team to sort through them. I do. I bring enterprise-grade discipline to your business, prove every tool in my own restaurant first, and make sure you never have to worry about the tech.
